Is the ORB strategy good for futures traders?

⌄

The ORB strategy can be useful for futures traders who want a repeatable way to trade the first major breakout after the market opens. Super Greedy is built for traders who want ORB rules automated across ES, NQ, MES, and MNQ.

What does ORB mean in trading?

⌄

ORB stands for Opening Range Breakout. The strategy defines the opening range high and low, then looks for price to break above or below that range with enough confirmation to enter a trade.

How does an ORB trade setup form?

⌄

An ORB setup forms after the opening range is created and price breaks above or below that range. It works best during high-volume futures sessions on liquid markets like ES, NQ, MES, and MNQ.
