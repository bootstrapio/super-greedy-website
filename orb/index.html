ORB strategy

ORB defines the first range, then trades the breakout.

The Opening Range Breakout (ORB) strategy identifies the high and low of the market's opening session, then enters a trade when price breaks above or below that range. Super Greedy converts this into a fully automated futures strategy with session filters, breakout validation, and predefined risk management.

  1. Opening range

    What is an Opening Range Breakout?

    An Opening Range Breakout (ORB) is a futures trading strategy where traders define the high and low of the market's opening period, then enter trades when price breaks above or below that range. It is commonly used to capture early-session momentum in liquid markets like ES and NQ.

    • Range alerts

Is the ORB strategy good for futures traders?

⌄

What does ORB mean in trading?

⌄

ORB stands for Opening Range Breakout. It is a strategy based on defining the opening session range and trading breakouts above or below that range.

⌄
