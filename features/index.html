Super Greedy Features

Every trading edge, built into one strategy.

Super Greedy is built for traders who want AI-driven signals, institutional zone detection, and automated execution without managing five different tools. The strategy combines real-time market analysis with configurable risk controls so you trade with confidence across futures markets.

Greedy AI Signals

High-probability entries on every setup

The AI model reads price action, volume, and market structure in real time and surfaces only the setups with the strongest confluence — so you stop guessing and start acting.

Supply & Demand Zones

Institutional zones, detected automatically

Key supply and demand zones are identified algorithmically based on momentum imbalances and volume patterns — plotted directly on the chart so you never miss a major reaction area.

Multi-Timeframe Analysis

Confirm setups across timeframes

Higher-timeframe bias is factored into every signal. Trades only trigger when the lower-timeframe entry aligns with the bigger picture — fewer false signals, higher confidence.

Automated Execution

Trade while you're away

Configure your entries, stops, and targets once. Super Greedy executes trades automatically based on your rules — no screen time required for every signal.

What You Can Do

Core capabilities inside Super Greedy

  • Receive real-time AI trade signals on ES, NQ, MES, MNQ, and configurable futures instruments.
  • View automatically detected supply and demand zones plotted directly on live charts.
  • Filter signals using higher-timeframe bias for stronger confluences and fewer noise trades.
  • Set configurable risk parameters including daily loss limits, drawdown thresholds, and position sizing.
  • Execute trades automatically or use signals manually — the strategy works both ways.
  • Review trade history, signal performance, and strategy metrics inside the dashboard.
