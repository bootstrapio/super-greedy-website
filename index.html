Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you should ask before you get started

Common questions about how Super Greedy works, what it trades, and how to get set up.

How does Super Greedy generate trade signals? +

Super Greedy uses a proprietary AI model that analyzes price action, volume, and market structure in real time to generate high-probability entry and exit signals across configured instruments.

What markets does Super Greedy support? +

Super Greedy is built for futures markets including ES, NQ, MES, and MNQ, with additional instruments configurable based on your trading setup.

Can I customize the strategy parameters? +

Yes. Risk per trade, position sizing, daily loss limits, target levels, and timeframe preferences are all configurable so the strategy fits your style and account rules.

Does Super Greedy work with prop firm rules? +

Yes. Super Greedy includes configurable risk parameters including daily loss limits, max drawdown thresholds, and position sizing controls that align with common prop firm evaluation rules.

How are supply and demand zones calculated? +

Supply and demand zones are identified algorithmically based on price structure, momentum imbalances, and volume patterns — the same areas institutional participants tend to transact.
