Super Greedy Use Cases

Built for traders who compete for an edge.

Super Greedy is most useful where signal quality and execution consistency directly affect your P&L. When you need reliable entries, disciplined risk management, and a strategy that runs without you watching every tick, the app removes a large amount of the guesswork.

Day Trading

Fast AI signals for intraday setups

Get real-time signals on ES, NQ, MES, and MNQ tuned for intraday price action, with built-in risk controls that keep you within daily limits on every session.

Prop Trading

Pass evaluations with rules-based entries

Configurable drawdown limits, position sizing, and daily loss thresholds make Super Greedy a natural fit for TopstepX and other prop firm evaluation environments.

Swing Trading

Overnight setups with HTF confirmation

Multi-timeframe analysis identifies setups aligned with daily and weekly structure, giving you higher-conviction entries on moves that play out over days.

Algorithmic Trading

Fully automated strategy execution

Configure entries, stops, and targets once and let Super Greedy execute the strategy automatically — no manual intervention required on every signal.
