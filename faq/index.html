Frequently asked questions

What is Super Greedy?

Super Greedy is automated futures trading software for ES, NQ, MES, and MNQ. It analyzes market conditions, follows your configured strategy settings, and executes trades automatically during market hours.

Does Super Greedy trade automatically without manual intervention?

Yes. Super Greedy detects setups, validates entries, and executes trades — including entry, stop placement, and take profit targets — automatically with no manual action required once configured.

How does Super Greedy detect setups?

Super Greedy evaluates live market conditions using its automated strategy engine and your configured rules to detect setups before execution.

What markets does Super Greedy support?

Super Greedy is built for futures markets including ES, NQ, MES, and MNQ, with additional instruments configurable based on your trading setup.

Can I customize the strategy parameters?

Yes. Risk per trade, position sizing, daily loss limits, target levels, and timeframe preferences are all configurable so the strategy fits your style and account rules.

Is Super Greedy compatible with TopstepX?

Yes. Super Greedy is designed to work with TopstepX and includes configurable daily loss limits, max drawdown thresholds, and position sizing controls that align with TopstepX evaluation rules.

Does Super Greedy work with prop firm rules?

Yes. Super Greedy includes configurable risk parameters including daily loss limits, max drawdown thresholds, and position sizing controls that align with common prop firm evaluation rules.
